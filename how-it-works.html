How It Works

Three steps between you and permit-ready HVAC reports.

Our Simple 3-Step Process

1

Upload Your Plans

Start by uploading your blueprints through our simple order form. We accept multiple formats to make things easy for you.

What We Accept:

  • Digital blueprints (PDF, CAD files)
  • Scanned floor plans
  • Photos of hand-drawn plans
  • Construction documents

What We Need:

  • Floor plan layout and square footage
  • Property location (city, state, zip)
  • Insulation details (R-values)
  • Window type and quantities
  • Ceiling height and any unusual features

Our order form walks you through everything step-by-step. If something is unclear, we'll reach out.

2

We Calculate Everything

Your project is assigned to one of our HVAC experts. We use Wrightsoft—the industry-standard software trusted by engineers and code officials.

What We Do:

  • Calculate room-by-room heating and cooling loads
  • Design complete ductwork for supply and return
  • Select and match equipment to your loads
  • Review every calculation for accuracy

Every report is calculated by a real person—no automated shortcuts. We take accuracy seriously because your HVAC system depends on it.

3

Download Your Reports

Your complete Manual J, D & S bundle is delivered digitally, ready for permits and installation.

What You Get:

  • Permit-ready Manual J load calculation report
  • Complete Manual D duct design and sizing
  • Manual S equipment specifications
  • ACCA-compliant and code-ready

Delivery Timeline:

  • Standard: 7 business days
  • Rush: Next business day (+$0.04/sq ft)

What You Get in Your Deliverables

Manual J Report

Room-by-room load calculations

Every space in your home analyzed. Heating and cooling BTU requirements detailed for summer and winter conditions. This is the foundation of proper HVAC sizing—no oversized or undersized systems.

Manual D Report

Complete ductwork design and sizing

Duct layout and specifications for supply and return air. Sizing calculated for optimal airflow and minimal friction loss. Includes schematic drawings your installer can follow exactly.

Manual S Report

Equipment selection and specifications

The right furnace, air conditioner, heat pump, or other equipment chosen and matched to your calculated loads. Equipment specs, capacity, and performance data included.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if I don't have formal blueprints?

No problem. We work with whatever you have. Hand-drawn sketches, photos of existing construction, rough floor plans—we've handled it all. What matters is that we understand the layout, square footage, and key details like insulation and windows. If something is unclear from what you provide, we'll contact you for clarification.

Can I make changes after you start?

Absolutely. If you need to modify the design after we begin calculations, just let us know. Small changes are usually quick to implement. Significant changes may affect the timeline, but we'll communicate clearly about any impacts.

What format are the reports delivered in?

Reports are delivered as PDF files, which are permit-ready and fully compatible with building departments across all 50 states. They're ready to print, submit for permits, and share with your contractor or installer immediately upon download.
