Why Sizing Matters

An Oversized System:

  • Short-cycling: Turns on and off constantly, wasting energy and wearing out the compressor faster.
  • Poor humidity control: Runs so little that it can't properly remove moisture from your home.
  • Accelerated wear: More starts and stops = more stress on components and a shorter lifespan.
  • Higher bills: Bigger isn't more efficient. You're paying for capacity you don't need.

A Properly Sized System:

  • Efficient operation: Runs at optimal capacity with fewer starts and stops, using less energy.
  • Better humidity removal: Runs longer per cycle, properly dehumidifying your home for comfort.
  • Extended lifespan: Normal cycling means less wear and a system that lasts 15+ years.
  • Lower bills: Right-sized efficiency means you pay for exactly what you need â no waste.
