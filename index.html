Common Questions

What is a Manual J calculation?

Manual J is the industry-standard method for calculating HVAC load for residential buildings. It accounts for climate, insulation, windows, occupancy, and appliances to determine exact heating and cooling requirements.

Why do I need Manual J, D, AND S?

Manual J calculates heating/cooling loads. Manual D sizes and designs the ductwork. Manual S selects the right equipment. Together, they ensure your HVAC system is properly sized, distributed, and equipped for peak performance and efficiency.

How long does it take?

Most reports are delivered within 7 business days. We also offer 48-hour rush delivery for an additional charge. Turnaround depends on plan clarity and complexity.

What do you need from me?

Send us your architectural plans or floor plans (PDF, image, or scanned). Include information about construction type, insulation levels, window counts, and your location. The more detail, the more accurate your calculation.
