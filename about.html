About Manual J USA

Accurate HVAC sizing, without the wait.

Our Story

We started Manual J USA because getting HVAC load calculations shouldn't be the hard part. Whether you're a contractor pulling permits or a homeowner planning a build, you deserve accurate reports delivered fast—without the runaround.

We're based in Boise, Idaho, and we serve contractors and homeowners in all 50 states. Every report is calculated by a real person using Wrightsoft—the industry-standard software trusted by HVAC engineers and code officials nationwide.

Our mission is simple: make it easy to get the HVAC sizing right. No shortcuts. No surprises. Just accurate, permit-ready reports that your team can trust.

What We Believe

Transparency Over Fine Print
